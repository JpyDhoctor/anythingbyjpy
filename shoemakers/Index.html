About Viceno

Founded in 2015, Viceno represents the pinnacle of artisanal shoemaking. Each pair is meticulously crafted by master shoemakers with decades of experience.

We use only the finest materials sourced from ethical suppliers, ensuring both quality and sustainability in every stitch.

Our process involves over 200 individual steps per pair, with each shoe requiring 25-30 hours of dedicated craftsmanship.
